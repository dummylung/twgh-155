OPEN TO THE PUBLIC, CELEBRATING A NEW MILESTONE IN CHARITY

Tung Wah Group of Hospitals(TWGHs), our Group will hold the "Tung Wah Group of Hospitals 155th Anniversary Community Fun Day" on November 22-23, 2025 (Saturday and Sunday) at TWGHs TungPo. Admission is free, and all Hong Kong citizens are cordially invited to join this grand event.

This Community Fun Day brings together various sectors under Tung Wah Group of Hospitals, including medical care, education, social services, historical and cultural preservation, public services, and fundraising services. There are more than 60 interactive booths. In addition to showcasing the various service achievements and development of TWGHs, the event also aims to promote the spirit of community harmony and charity. It is suitable for the whole family to enjoy together and share the joyfulness of Tung Wah Group of Hospitals' 155th Anniversary.

Fire Dragon Dance – POKFULAM Village Fire Dragon Association
Pok Fu Lam Village holds a century-old Fire Dragon Dance every Mid-Autumn Festival, where villagers parade to pray for blessings and strengthen community bonds. In celebration of the TWGHs’ 155th anniversary, the Group held the "Man Mo Parade cum Autumn Sacrificial Rites," led by sacred palanquins and accompanied by a 100 feet fire dragon. After the event, the dragon’s head and part of its body were preserved and will be displayed at the TWGHs 155th Anniversary Fun Day, allowing the public to experience wielding the dragon and perpetuating the craftsmanship and dance traditions.
Event details
Date: 22nd&23rd, November 2025
Time: 10:00am to 7:00pm
Location: G/F TWGHs 150th Anniversary Board of Directors 2020/2021 Exhibition Foyer, TungPo

E Major Ensemble
The E Major Ensemble was established in 2016 and is one of Hong Kong’s few western music orchestras for seniors. Members are people aged 50 or above who are passionate about music, including retirees. Through training and stage performances, the Ensemble promotes the belief that “Dreams Without Limits, Music Without Boundaries.” At present, the Ensemble consists of 67 members who play string, woodwind, brass, percussion, and keyboard instruments.

Chinese Drumming Performance by Persons of Diverse Abilities
The Eagle Drum Team was founded in 2007 and is Hong Kong's first Chinese drum team formed by visually impaired elders and individuals with mental disabilities, hearing impairments, autism, and severe physical disabilities. Through drumming practice and public performances, the members build synergy. Documentary “Snuggle” Screening
The documentary “Snuggle”, produced by Tung Wah Group of Hospitals and directed by Wong Siu-pong, delicately presents the differences in values regarding “life and death” across two generations. Through three families, it explores the similarities and constraints between two societal generations, the bonds of trust between couples, and teaches us how to face key life transitions. One of the sessions features a special appearance by the director, witnessing these profound stories.

Arrangements for Adverse Weather

Special Arrangements under Tropical Cyclone and Black Rainstorm Warning Signals:
Visitors are advised to note the following special arrangements implemented by Tung Wah Group of Hospitals under tropical cyclone (commonly known as ‘typhoon’) or black rainstorm warning signals.

  • Should the Hong Kong Observatory maintain a Tropical Cyclone Warning Signal No. 8 or above, or a Black Rainstorm Warning Signal at 7:00 am on the day of the event, the ‘morning session’ of the event will be cancelled.
  • Should the Hong Kong Observatory maintain a Tropical Cyclone Warning Signal No. 8 or above, or a Black Rainstorm Warning Signal at 11:00 am on the day of the event, the ‘afternoon session’ will likewise be cancelled.
Tung Wah Group of Hospitals will disseminate subsequent updates via its official Facebook and Instagram. Please stay tuned.
